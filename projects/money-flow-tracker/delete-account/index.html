Account Deletion

Request deletion of your Money Flow Tracker account and all associated data.

If you want to delete your Money Flow Tracker account, please follow one of the methods below. We will verify your request and permanently remove your account data within 30 days.

How to request deletion

  • Open the Money Flow Tracker app and use the Delete Account option (if available)
  • Email mft-core@googlegroups.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request"
Please include:
  • The email address associated with your account.

Data that will be deleted

  • Account information
  • Expense and income records
  • Budgets and categories
  • Cloud backup data stored in Firebase/Firestore
  • Profile information

Data retention

Account and associated data are permanently deleted within 30 days of request verification. Certain records may be retained if required by law, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.

Contact

For account deletion requests, email the MFT Core Team at mft-core@googlegroups.com.
