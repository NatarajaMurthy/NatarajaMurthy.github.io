Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously

Last Updated: June 15, 2026

Money Flow Tracker ("MFT", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Money Flow Tracker application.

Information We Collect

The application may collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Income records
  • Expense records
  • Account names and balances
  • Budget information
  • Transaction categories
  • Transaction notes
  • Savings goals
  • Receipt images uploaded by users (optional)

How We Use Information

  • Provide application functionality
  • Generate reports and analytics
  • Improve user experience
  • Improve application performance and stability
  • Provide future AI-powered financial insights

Third-Party Services

The application may use third-party services that may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob (if advertisements are enabled)

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

Data Retention

User data is retained only as long as necessary to provide application functionality and comply with applicable legal requirements.

Children's Privacy

Money Flow Tracker is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

  • Review your information
  • Delete your application data
  • Uninstall the application at any time

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
